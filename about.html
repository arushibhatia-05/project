About the Team

Our Mission

We aim to analyze and provide insights on ski resort dynamics to enhance tourist experiences and operational strategies. As passionate skiers, we face the problem of trying to find the perfect resort for our needs, especially as college students that are looking for budget friendly options. We wanted to conduct this analysis to support people that are also in our position to ensure people are thoughtfully picking the ski resort of their choice, and consider the important factors when it comes to finding the perfect mountain.
